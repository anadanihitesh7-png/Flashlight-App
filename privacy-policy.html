🔦 Flashlight App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Flashlight App is committed to protecting your privacy.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

📋 Information Collection and Use

Flashlight App works entirely offline. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or processed on external servers. The app only accesses your device's flashlight hardware to function correctly.

💾 Data Storage

Flashlight App does not store any personal data. No user information, usage data, or activity history is saved.

🔐 Permissions

The app requires only one permission:

  • Camera / Flashlight Permission – Used solely to turn the flashlight ON and OFF.
Important: This permission is not used to take photos, record videos, or access camera data.

🚫 Third-Party Services

Flashlight App does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking tools
  • Third-party SDKs that collect user data

🛡️ Security

Since Flashlight App operates entirely offline and collects no data, your information is inherently secure. No data is transmitted over the internet, eliminating the risk of data breaches or unauthorized access.

📝 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if new features are added or to comply with legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected in the app store listing or within the app.
